Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?▼

Donations to SenseAid are tax-deductible. We will provide you with a donation receipt for your records. Please consult with your tax adviser for specific details.

How is my donation used?▼

Your donation directly funds platform development, server costs, accessibility research, and community support. We maintain transparent reporting of all expenditures.

Can I make a recurring donation?▼

Yes! You can set up monthly or annual donations. This helps us plan long-term initiatives and provide consistent support to our community.

What payment methods do you accept?▼

We accept all major payment methods including bank transfers, card payments, and digital wallets. For Nigerian donors, we support direct bank transfers and mobile money.

Can I donate anonymously?▼

Absolutely! You can choose to remain anonymous during checkout. Your privacy is important to us.
